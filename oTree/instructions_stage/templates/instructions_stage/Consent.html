Thank you for choosing to take this task.

This is an academic study of the Technion University, in which we examine how people make decisions. There are no known risks or benefits from participating in this study. It will take about 15 minutes to complete the task.

There are no risks involved with participation in this study. The data collected will be used for research purposes only. Your participation is completely voluntary, and you are free to withdraw at any time. However, if you decide to terminate your participation before the end of the study, you will not be compensated. We will not analyze any information that can identify you personally, and any information you provide will remain anonymous and will be reported only on the aggregate level.

The instructions for the task, and the rules for the bonus payment will appear on the next screen. Read them carefully and make sure you understand the "rules of the game." Among other things, your bonus payment will depend on your understanding of these instructions. A failure to read the instructions may result in you not being able to complete the task.

Important! To get the Prolific completion code, as well as your base payment and bonus, you will have to complete the task without breaks. In case of lengthy idle time, the task will automatically stop, and you will not be able to take it again. This task cannot be taken from a mobile device.

If you have any questions concerning this task, please email to Kinneret Teodorescu at kinnerett@technion.ac.il

If you agree to these terms, check the CONSENT button otherwise you cannot start the task. If you want to exit, just close the browser or tab.
